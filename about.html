About Our Association

Six decades of friendship, mentorship and giving back.

Our Story

Founded in 1962, the ShivMaya State College Alumni Association began as a small circle of graduates determined to stay connected. Over the decades it has grown into a global community of more than twelve thousand members spanning forty-two countries.

From funding scholarships to mentoring the next generation of students, our alumni continue to shape the institution they once called home. This page tells the story of who we are and where we're headed.

🎯

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections among graduates, support the academic excellence of our college, and create meaningful opportunities for alumni to give back and grow together.

🌟

Our Vision

A vibrant, engaged global network where every alumnus feels a sense of belonging and pride, empowering students and society for generations to come.

Our Journey

  1. 1962 — Foundation

    The association is established by the first graduating batch.

  2. 1985 — First Global Chapter

    Alumni open the first overseas chapter, connecting members worldwide.

  3. 2001 — Scholarship Fund

    The endowment fund is launched, supporting hundreds of students since.

  4. 2015 — Digital Network

    An online platform connects more than 10,000 members instantly.

  5. 2024 — 60+ Years Strong

    Celebrating six decades with a record-breaking reunion and giving campaign.
